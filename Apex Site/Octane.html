Octane: High-Speed Daredevil

Crazy, death defying, stim addict, Octane, sets a record time in a race by launching himself across the course with a grenade resulting in robotic leg replacements. This highly mobile daredevil flies across the map outrunning and outgunning all opponents in his way.

Real Name: Octavio Silva

Age: 24

Home Planet: Psamathe

Passive Ability: Swift Mend - Automatically restores health over time.

Tactical Ability: Stim - Move 30% faster for six seconds. Costs health to use

Ultimate Ability: Launch Pad - Deploy a jump pad that catapults teammates through the air

Heirloom: Butterfly Knife
